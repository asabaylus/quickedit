Tips

A few suggestions.

  1. A sentence with punctuation is better than a traditional passphrase or password.
  2. You can encrypt text more than once. Just click the encrypt button a few times. To decrypt, click the decrypt button multiple times till you see your original text.
  3. You could change the passphrase between multiple encryptions. Reverse the sequence of the passphases when decrypting.
